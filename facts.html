Raindrop Shape

Raindrops are not teardrop-shaped. As they fall, air resistance flattens them, making them look more like **hamburger buns**.

Cloud Weight

World's Driest Spot

The driest place on Earth is not a hot desert, but the **McMurdo Dry Valleys in Antarctica**, where some areas haven't seen rain or snow for millions of years.

Fastest Wind Gust

The fastest non-tornadic wind speed ever recorded was **253 mph** (408 km/h) on Barrow Island, Australia, during Tropical Cyclone Olivia in 1996.

Fog is a Cloud

**Fog** is simply a **cloud that forms at or near the ground**. It forms when the air cools and the water vapor condenses into tiny droplets.

Tornado on Jupiter

The **Great Red Spot** on Jupiter is a massive, long-lasting hurricane, or **cyclone**, that is larger than the entire planet Earth.

Windiest US State

The state in the US with the highest average annual wind speed is **South Dakota**.

Snowflake Uniqueness

While they come in many forms, it is said that **no two snowflakes are exactly alike**, as the unique conditions in the atmosphere create an infinite number of crystalline patterns.
